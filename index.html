LUDO ROYALE

Custom-ruled online multiplayer · 2 dice · mandatory captures

Custom Rules
  • All 4 tokens start active. No "roll-6-to-start".
  • Two dice; reduced to one when you have 3 tokens in your home lane.
  • Both dice must be used (separately, or combined on one token).
  • Bonus turn only on double 6 — never for captures or finishing.
  • Captured tokens respawn at their start (not in jail).
  • If you can capture, you MUST. Skipping a capture sends your guilty token back to start.
  • Exact roll needed to reach home. Home lane is uncapturable.
